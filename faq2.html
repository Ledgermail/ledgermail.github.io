FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

Q :

What is a Blockchain based Decentralized Email Solution?

A :

Blockchain based email solution leverages Blockchain technology as its underlying mechanism and operates in a decentralized way, where there is no single authority to control email service. Current email transfer protocols such as IMAP/SMTP are replaced with Blockchain technology, where messages, files and attachments are stored in shared ledgers of blockchain which are distributed across the network.

Q :

What is difference between Legacy email providers and LedgerMail?

A :

Legacy email providers such as Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo etc. uses IMAP/SMTP as email transfer protocols and have centralized governance over email ecosystem.
LedgerMail replaces these protocols with Blockchain technology and builds decentralized email ecosystem with no single point of control or failure.
