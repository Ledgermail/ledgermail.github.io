Why Decentralized Email Solution?

1. Because current email ecosystem is obsolete, hackable and cumbersome in nature:

Current email transfer protocols such as IMAP/SMTP were designed & developed in 1982 and were last updated in 2008. Having such an outdated email transfer protocols resulted as an easy target for sophisticated hackers.

94% of cyber attacks are carried out through emails where as $26 Billion of monetary loss has been complained in past 2 years.

2. Because user privacy is getting compromised through current email providers:

Email service providers read, process and target ads to it's users. Making meaningful use of email data is the way free services make money. Read more on email privacy

3. Because, even encrypted email services can be hacked:

Current encryption only provides network level encryption, payload data or actual email content still remains unencrypted and makes it vulnerable to breach.

4. Because centralized storage is failing to secure user data:

Having centralized storage for information can be vulnerable to single point of failure which can suffer a catastrophic failure, accidentally burn to ground or be seized by a third party.

What is LedgerMail

A Decentralized Solution to transfer Secure, Private and Encrypted Emails

The Blockchain Network is an underlying infrastructure that supports LedgerMail service which operates in a decentralized ecosystem. LedgerMail uses DPoS algorithm which provides faster transactions and less energy consumption.

LedgerMail replaces existing email transfer protocols such as IMAP/SMTP with Blockchain Technology where messages are stored on shared ledgers in a decentralized way.

LedgerMail encrypts emails on the client side with recipients public key. The emails sent on the network are verified & authenticated for the correct recipient. This avoids any hacks of modified headers and spam emails possible in existing email protocols.

LedgerMail uses asymmetric encryption which means only sender & receiver can read emails with their private keys resulting in transcendent security, enhanced privacy and cryptographic encryption.

Disruptive, Immutable, Secure, Highly Customizable & Cost Effective Solution which will replace the traditional way of email exchange with the Power of Blockchain.

Ease of Use

Seamless & Hassle-Free User Experience

Unlike other email security providers where users are supposed to compromise with their seamless user experience, change their existing email ID or migrate their emails to new platform; LedgerMail provides seamless & hassle-free experience.

LedgerMail integrates seamlessly with your browser using a chrome extension. The extension is an offline application which works as a secure vault to store the public and private keys of the user account.

Chromium based browser plug-in allows users to integrate with existing email platforms O365/GSuite while keeping same email ID and user experience.

LedgerMail team can also develop customized UI matching user web application and ecosystem.

In this way, users can have seamless & hassle-free experience with enhanced security, privacy, encryption and the Power of Blockchain.
